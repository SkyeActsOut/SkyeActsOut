# Projects

## Your Turn To Thrive · @YourTurnToThrive · Ongoing
![Your Turn To Thrive](https://cdn.yttt.org/images/Banner/NaoBanner.jpg)
Your Turn To Thrive is a visual novel and stage play sequel to [Your Turn To Die](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2067780/Your_Turn_To_Die_Death_Game_By_Majority/) taking place 6 months after massacre ending with Nao Egokoro as the protagonist. Her and Sara Chidouin start over in a fresh environment as they continue to recover from the events of the death game, forming new bonds of mutual support, and unraveling the mystery of their new home. The source code can be found [here](https://git.yttt.org/YourTurnToThrive/YTTT)!
